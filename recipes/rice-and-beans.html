Rice and Beans

Description

A delicious symphony of rice and beans with notes of profound flavour - a treat for all those privilleged enought ot try it.

Supremely adaptable, it remains delicious no matter what ingredients are used. Rice and beans is not a mere dish; it is an avenue for self-exploration.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Avocado
  • Hummus
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce

  • CREATIVITY!

Steps

  1. Prepare the desired amount of rice
  2. Filter desired beans through a seev over the sink
  3. Pour the filtered beans onto the rice
  4. Cut up the desired amount of onions

    DO NOT place them into the bowl at this step

  5. Microwave the bowl containing the rice as well as the beans for one minute.
  6. Scoop one tablespoon of hummus into the bowl
  7. Trasfer the diced onions into the bowl
  8. Scoop and entire avocado into the bowl
  9. Finally, finish the dish off with your desired amount of soy sauce

And your rice and beans is done!
