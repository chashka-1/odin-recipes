Homemade Green Pasta

Description

A recipe combining two of the best prodcuts at Trader Joe's, blended together for a full gastronomic experience.

Very difficult to make; if you fail on your first attempt, do not distress!

Ingredients

  • Kale linguini pasta from Trader Joe's
  • Vegan pesto from Trader Joe's
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Place half a pack of linguini pasta into the pot
  3. Lower the heat on the stove to four
  4. Let the pasta sit until it becomes softer and more malleable, but not until it's completely squishy/sticky
  5. Use a seev to pour out only the pasta into the boul
  6. Scoop out one and a half tablespoons of vegan pesto into the bowl
  7. Pour the desired amount of soy sauce
  8. Thoroughly mix until every string of linguini is covered in the pesto

And your green pasta is done!
